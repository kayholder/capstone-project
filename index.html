JUST FOR THE SUMMER

Just for the Summer is a romantic comedy novel by Abby Jimenez about Emma and Justin, two people who share a curse that causes their dates to find their soulmates in later relationships. To break the curse, they decide to date for four dates, share a kiss, and then break up. However, things don't go as planned.
